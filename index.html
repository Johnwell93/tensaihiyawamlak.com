About Us

Tensai Hiyaw Amlak is a vibrant and welcoming Eritrean church community located in the heart of Zürich, Switzerland. Rooted in the Holy Trinity, we are dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth and guiding everyone to focus on Jesus Christ as our Savior.
