About Us

Mahber Tensai Hiyaw Amlak is a vibrant and welcoming church community based in the heart of Zürich, Switzerland. We are dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth and fostering a sense of togetherness among our members.

Our mission is to connect people to God through worship, prayer, and meaningful fellowship. Whether you're seeking a spiritual home or visiting for the first time, you will find a supportive community that embraces faith, love, and purpose.

We offer a wide range of activities, from Bible study and prayer meetings to engaging youth programs and outreach services. Join us and become a part of our journey to grow in faith together!
